# Marché Freshco — Brand Kit

Generated direction for the website build. Boards are in this folder (`01`–`05`).
Use this spec as the source of truth for tokens; the PNGs are visual reference.

## Brand idea
**A fresh neighbourhood world-market.** Friendly, multicultural, trustworthy, great value.
"Fresh" is the core promise. Bilingual FR/EN: **Marché** (market) + **Freshco** (fresh + co).

**Tagline:** *Fresh, every day.* / *Frais, chaque jour.*

## Logo
- **Wordmark:** "MARCHÉ FRESHCO" — stacked (Marché over Freshco) or horizontal, in a friendly geometric sans, forest green.
- **Symbol (recommended):** a circular **fruit + leaf emblem** — orange citrus/segment wedges cradled by green leaves forming a ring (see `05-logo-construction.png` / `03-applications.png`). Reads as fresh produce + "market basket."
- **Icon/badge:** the emblem alone, or an **"F" mark** in an orange disc for app/favicon (see `01`, `03`).
- Always keep clear space ≥ the height of the leaf; min wordmark width ~120px on web.

Rule: **green is dominant, orange is the single repeating accent**, red only for sale/price, lime sparingly. Cream is the canvas (not stark white) — matches the "warm fresh-market" feel.

## Typography
- **Display / headings:** a warm geometric sans — recommend **Poppins** or **Outfit** (SemiBold/Bold). Friendly, round, modern; works bilingually.
- **Body / UI:** **Inter** (Regular/Medium) for clean readability.
- **Optional accent:** a hand/marker style for "fresh price" tags echoing the in-store fluorescent price cards (e.g. **Caveat**) — use very sparingly.

## Imagery direction
- Real, bright, close-up **produce photography** (the store's strength): apples, peppers, herbs, citrus on wood market tables.
- Warm natural light, cream/wood backdrops, shallow depth of field.
- Avoid sterile stock-supermarket shots; lean into abundant, colourful, hand-stacked market feel.
