## 2. Who they serve (positioning)

A neighbourhood **international grocery** in Pierrefonds serving a multicultural West-Island
clientele — strongest signals are **South Asian (Indian / Pakistani / Bangladeshi)**,
**Middle Eastern / halal**, plus **African & Caribbean** (Vita Malt, plantains, cassava/yam).
Islamic decorative art on the back wall confirms a halal-focused Muslim customer base.

Effectively three stores in one:
1. **Fresh fruit & vegetable market** (front of store, the "fresh" in the name)
2. **International dry-goods grocery** (rice, lentils, flours, spices, tea, snacks)
3. **Cosmetics / beauty + household** corner (legacy "Marché et Cosmétiques")

## 3. Department-by-department inventory (from the 360° tour)

### 🍎 Fresh Produce — front of store (the hero section)
Wooden tiered display tables + open refrigerated wall case.
- **Fruits:** bananas, plantains, green apples (Granny Smith), red apples, yellow apples,
  pears, oranges / clementines, grapes (dark), assorted seasonal fruit.
- **Vegetables / roots:** potatoes, cauliflower, garlic, onions, cassava / yam (long brown roots),
  tomatoes, bell & hot peppers, eggplant, fresh leafy greens & herbs (coriander etc.).
- Hand-written fluorescent price tags everywhere → **value / fresh-market** feel.

### 🥫 Dry goods & staples (main aisles)
- **Grains & pulses:** bags of rice (basmati etc.), lentils / dals, chickpeas, beans.
- **Flours:** atta / wheat flour, specialty flours.
- **Cooking fats:** ghee tins, cooking oils.
- Pasta, canned vegetables & beans, canned fish.

### 🫖 Tea, snacks & packaged
- Wide tea selection (boxed teas — major South-Asian brands).
- Biscuits & cookies (Ritz etc.), chips & namkeen / snacks, confectionery.

### 🥛 Dairy & milk powders
- **Nestlé NIDO** milk powder tins, Peak milk powder, evaporated / condensed milk.

### 🧃 Refrigerated drinks wall
- Juices (Tampico, Rubicon-style mango/tropical), **YOP** yogurt drinks,
  **Vita Malt** malt beverage, smoothies, dairy drinks, soft drinks in glass-door coolers (Coca-Cola).

### 🧹 Household & cleaning
- Aluminum foil, paper towels, detergents (large bags), Comet, cleaning supplies, disposables.

### 🥖 Bakery
- Glass display case with fresh breads / buns / pastries near the counter.

### 💄 Cosmetics & beauty / health
- Legacy cosmetics corner (hair & beauty products) — a differentiator vs. plain grocers.

### 🧊 Frozen / coolers
- Glass-door upright freezers & coolers (frozen foods + cold drinks).

## 5. Brand signals observed (input to the brand kit)

- **Name:** Marché Freshco — bilingual-friendly (FR "Marché" + EN "Freshco"); "Fresh" is the core idea.
- **Storefront:** red awning / roof line; colourful fruit & spice murals on the window glass.
- **In-store palette:** warm produce colours (reds, greens, yellows, orange) against a clean white interior.
- **Personality:** fresh, friendly, multicultural, trustworthy, great-value neighbourhood market.
- **No existing logo/wordmark** found → green-field brand kit opportunity.
- Suggested direction: **fresh-market** identity — green (fresh/halal-friendly) + warm
  orange/red accent, clean modern wordmark, leaf / produce motif, bilingual FR/EN lockup.
